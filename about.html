Welcome to the College Predictor. This is a Data Science project where we predict colleges for students based on their marks using a model. The model is trained on the admission procedures of various NIT and IIT colleges.
First, we analyze data from different colleges and their admission statistics for various programs. Then the model suggests top colleges for students based on their features to help them get admission.
